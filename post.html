Announcement 3 July, 2020
Introducing the TBCC Wallet

The TBCC Labs introduces the TBCC Wallet — an easy-to-use and convenient wallet that supports leading cryptocurrencies BEP2 on the Binance Chain. Now you can start to work with crypto assets through our TBCC app on your IOS or Android device.

The wallet works seamlessly with the Binance DEX protocols, that allows you to make instant and quick deals on decentralized markets.

The service is absolutely safe and anonymous — the TBCC service will never get the access to your personal information. The TBCC Wallet always keeps your data secret and secure.

One of the main questions that all the people deal with when they decide to buy crypto assets is what to do next with my cryptocurrency? In most cases, with other wallets, the only thing that you will be able to do is to buy and invest. That is it. If you want to make something with the crypto assets that you gained — you had to search for another platform that would help you. But you will not deal with such difficulties through the TBCC Wallet.

So, what you can do after buying crypto assets with the TBCC Wallet:

  • Make contactless payments through the app on your phone;
  • Make payments from your TBCC Wallet via QR-code;
  • Make online payments;
  • Make payments via NFC

The TBCC Wallet is a modern and convenient blockchain wallet for everyone. Start to use it now!
